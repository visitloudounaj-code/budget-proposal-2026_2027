ESTO — Educational Seminar for Tourism Organizations

August 23–26, 2026 Philadelphia, PA U.S. Travel Association
Priority Tier 1 Top Recommendation

ESTO is the flagship industry conference for U.S. destination marketing organizations. It is open exclusively to destination professionals — DMO, CVB, and state tourism office staff — with no outside industry sectors permitted to attend. Every session, every roundtable, every networking conversation is with someone doing the same kind of work I do. The agenda maps directly to my job description, and Philadelphia is reachable by Amtrak for under $200 roundtrip.

Format That Sets ESTO Apart

The 2025 edition in Phoenix introduced the AI Playground — a hands-on, interactive activation space where destination marketers explored practical AI applications for visitor engagement, campaign automation, and content strategy. Being ranked first no longer guarantees clicks — being cited inside the AI Overview is the new goal, and that requires content structured fundamentally differently from traditional SEO. brightonSEO is where the practitioners solving this problem are publishing their work first. The DMO industry will get to this conversation second.
